# Metacognition & Planning Bibliography


DeepMind (2026)
Google DeepMind. Measuring Progress Toward AGI: A Cognitive Taxonomy. (2026).

Levels of AGI Framework
Morris, M. R., et al. Levels of AGI for Operationalizing Progress on the Path to AGI. (2023). arXiv:2311.02462.

### METACOGNITION

"Taxonomy of Metacognition"
Tarricone, P. (2011). The Taxonomy of Metacognition. Routledge.

Metacognitive Awareness Inventory (MAI)
Schraw, G., & Dennison, R. S. (1994). Assessing metacognitive awareness. Contemporary Educational Psychology.
(Note: You listed Dunlosky and Metcalfe here. While they didn't author the MAI, they wrote the highly cited textbook Dunlosky, J., & Metcalfe, J. (2008). Metacognition. Sage Publications.)

Metacognition and Cognitive Monitoring
Flavell, J. H. (1979). Metacognition and cognitive monitoring. American Psychologist.

"How to Measure Metacognition" (Efficiency, Sensitivity, and Accuracy)
Fleming, S. M., & Lau, H. (2014). How to measure metacognition. Frontiers in Human Neuroscience.

Metacognition Monitoring in Decision-Making
Yeung, N., & Summerfield, C. (2012). Metacognition in human decision-making: confidence and error monitoring. Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society B.

Metalearning "Learning to Learn"
Binz, M., et al. (2024). Meta-learned models of cognition. Behavioral and Brain Sciences.

Self-Regulated Learning (SRL)
Wang, J., et al. (2021). Impact of self-regulated learning on online learning. (This points to a general scholar search, as multiple distinct papers under Wang in 2021 explore SRL).

Empirical JOLs (Judgments of Learning)
Arbuckle, T. Y., & Cuddy, L. L. (1969). Discrimination of item strength at time of presentation. Journal of Experimental Psychology.

Metamemory Framework
Nelson, T. O., & Narens, L. (1990). Metamemory: A theoretical framework and new findings. The Psychology of Learning and Motivation.

Self-Knowledge of Personality
Vazire, S., & Carlson, E. N. (2010). Self-knowledge of personality. Social and Personality Psychology Compass.

Humanity's Last Exam
Phan, L., et al. (2025). Humanity's Last Exam. Center for AI Safety.

DeepSearchQA
Haas, L., et al. (2026). DeepSearchQA: Bridging the Comprehensiveness Gap for Deep Research Agents. arXiv preprint.

Source Monitoring Framework (SMF)
Johnson, M. K., Hashtroudi, S., & Lindsay, D. S. (1993). Source monitoring. Psychological Bulletin.

Learning from Errors
Metcalfe, J. (2017). Learning from errors. Annual Review of Psychology.

Executive Functions (EFs)
Diamond, A. (2013). Executive functions. Annual Review of Psychology.

Planning Processes
Mattar, M. G., & Lengyel, M. (2022). Planning in the brain. Neuron.

### AGI 

Legg, S., & Hutter, M. (2007). Universal Intelligence: A Definition of Machine Intelligence. Minds and Machines.

Lake, B. M., Ullman, T. D., Tenenbaum, J. B., & Gershman, S. J. (2017). Building machines that learn and think like people. Behavioral and Brain Sciences.

Goertzel, B. (2014). Artificial General Intelligence: Concept, State of the Art, and Future Prospects. Journal of Artificial General Intelligence.
